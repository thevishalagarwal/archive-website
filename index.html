    About me

    Hi! I am a final year undergraduate at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, India, pursuing major in Electronics and Electrical Engineering and minor in Computer Science. I am advised by Prof. Ashish Anand for my undergraduate thesis. I am working on applied deep learning in Genomics. Specifically on sequence models for splice site prediction and identification of complex splicing signals which influence splicing and hence discover important motifs.

    My interest lies in the field of Deep Learning and Reinforcement learning and I have research experience in Computational Biology and Computer Vision.

    In the past, I have worked on Image Contrast Enhancement in an internship at Computational Vision and Fuzzy Systems Lab, Hanyang University, South Korea, under the supervision of Prof. Frank Chung-Hoon Rhee. Also, I did a summer internship at NVIDIA where I worked with the GPU Performance Verification Team for improving latency analysis in the performance simulation environment.
